Hybrid Filtering Architecture

Most apps use simple filtering. CineRec combines two powerful methods:

  • Content-Based Filtering: Analyzes movie attributes (Director, Cast, Genre) to find similar matches.
  • Collaborative Filtering: Leverages user behavior patterns to predict what you'll like based on similar users.
